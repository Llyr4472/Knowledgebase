    Bandwidth vs Throughput

    Bandwidth vs Throughput

    Jan 21, 20251 min read

    Bandwidth

    It is the total capacity of a network to transfer data over an interval of time. It is measured in kbps, mbps, gbps.

    Throughput

    It is also the capacity of network to transfer data but also considers latency and other factors.

    It means bandwidth is how much it can transfer and throughput is how much it is able to transfer with a time interval due to factors such as latency and data loss.
